PAIN (N1–N5) is the FedRAMP Rev5 VDR Potential Agency Impact: the CVSS impact vector (C/I/A) — with each in-scope dimension raised to High when CISA Vulnrichment technical impact is "total" — weighted by the asset archetype's CR/IR/AR requirements and agency scope. Remediation is the FedRAMP VDR-TFR-PVR deadline = matrix[Certification Class][PAIN][LEV/IRV], where LEV = EPSS ≥ 0.50, active exploitation, or the FedRAMP unauthenticated-automation floor (internet-reachable plus AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N), and IRV = internet-reachable. A high-confidence chainable-entrypoint badge requires an active finding on an internet-accessible asset plus a high-confidence CVE execution classification; exposed possible candidates remain visible as possible. The flag is informational and does not alter IRV or downstream findings.
